3. Description of Service

Time2Split is a bill-splitting application that allows you to:

  • Scan receipts using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract items and prices
  • Create and manage shared bills among participants
  • Assign items to individual participants or split items evenly
  • Calculate proportional tip distribution
  • Determine minimum payment transactions needed to settle debts
  • Save and reuse participant groups
  • Support 30 international currencies
